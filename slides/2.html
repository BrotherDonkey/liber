What is a design system?

It's not only

  • a style guide.
  • a css framework.
  • a set of Figma plugins.

It's all of those things, working together.

... and ...

  • It's a mature process that let's things that make sense into the system.
  • It's improved communication between design and engineering.
  • It's a highly interconnected series of systems, which ensures that things stay up to date.
