You get a design ...

... (hypothetically) it contains things that do not exist in the design system.

This is how to get something into Atlas.

  1. Visit the design system to look or ask team member who might know.
  2. Begin a discussion @ https://github.com/microsoft/atlas-design/discussions
  3. Get quorum from the design folks. Get design signoff.
  4. Lead designers will approve the new feature for Atlas. (Or they won't, and they'll communicate with the designer.)
  5. Make a work item to add it to Atlas. Write well and thoroughly. Parent it under the feature you're working on.
  6. Write the code, or wait until someone else has time to write the code. Write a changeset. Do a PR. Get review.
  7. Changesets
  8. Well ... you still need to get that change into docs-ui.
